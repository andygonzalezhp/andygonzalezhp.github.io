Projects

Spatial Transcriptomics

Read the reserach proposal here.

This projects overarchiing aim is to incorporate spatial context in the diffusion map we use for Diffusion Spacial Pseudotime (DSPT) inference. This is done by including the Euclidean distance x0 and y0. This represents how two cells are close in terms of biological differentiation since they would also be spatially close.
